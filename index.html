01

Overview

GT:NH Processing+ is an expansion addon for GregTech: New Horizons. It's not trying to fix GTNH or change what it is. GTNH is already great. This mod just adds more of what it already does well: new processing chains, new machines, and mechanics that reward players who go deeper without punishing everyone else.

There's content at every tier. Some of it is optional and rewards players who build it. Some of it gates progression the way GTNH naturally does. All of it should feel like it belongs in the pack.

Core Principle

Every piece of content should make a player feel like their effort was worth it. Something new greets you at each tier. Nothing exists just to add steps.

What this mod is

More of what GTNH does. New chains using existing machines. New machines that solve problems players already feel. Content that feels like it was always supposed to be there.

What this mod is not

A difficulty overhaul. A world gen mod. A replacement for anything that already exists. Processing+ runs alongside GTNH, not against it.

02

Design Philosophy

These principles drive every design decision. Content that satisfies none of them doesn't belong. There's no strict checklist.

01
Branching Routes

No single optimal path. Every branch has different costs and tradeoffs. The best choice changes as your base evolves.

02
Byproducts Matter

No output is throwaway. Every byproduct should have a use somewhere in this mod or in base GTNH.

03
Infrastructure Pressure

Content is designed to be undersized when first built. The game rewards players who expand, not players who stop at the minimum.

04
Layered Depth

New tiers add layers to existing content. Early infrastructure stays relevant and useful later.

05
Named Lines

Critical materials graduate to dedicated continuous pipelines with internal loops. The platline model.

06
Diverse Challenges

Not every piece of content is a throughput problem. Some test layout, some test automation, some test routing. Variety matters.

07
Web of Dependencies

Nothing stands alone. Everything touches at least two other things as a consumer, a producer, or both.

08
Grounded Logic

Reactions and processes are based in real industrial logic. The game simplifies heavily but the reasoning should still feel satisfying.

On Difficulty

Difficulty comes from infrastructure demands, not step count. A three-step chain requiring a new multiblock and two continuous inputs is harder than a ten-step chain in one machine. Respect the difficulty GTNH already has, don't undercut it or pile on top of it.

On Tier Transitions

Every tier transition should have a moment. Something new that greets the player and makes the grind feel worth it. Content at each tier should solve problems players already feel, not create new arbitrary ones.

03

Content by Tier

This is the living part of the document. Each tier gets its own block and features get added as they're fully designed. Status tags show where each feature is at.

MV
Early Game
First multiblock. Before the EBF's full pipeline.
Basic Oxygen Furnace (BOF) Designing
MultiblockOptional
A small MV multiblock that converts hot charged iron into steel using a continuous oxygen lance. Cheaper than the EBF but better at steel specifically. Solves the pre-EBF steel bottleneck without removing the challenge of getting there. Stays relevant as a steel specialist even after EBF is online.
  • Cheaper to build than EBF. Can be running before EBF materials are stockpiled.
  • Needs continuous oxygen. Real infrastructure investment.
  • Three carbon content modes via Alloy Smelter preheating. Each with real tradeoffs.
Iron Preheating and Carbon Tuning Designing
ChainOptional
The prep step that feeds the BOF. Iron ingots plus varying amounts of carbon in the Alloy Smelter give you hot charged iron with three different carbon levels. Each feeds into the BOF with different results.
  • Low carbon: fastest BOF, highest steel per time. Wasteful but rapid.
  • Standard carbon: balanced output.
  • High carbon: 3-4x slower but fully recycles carbon and oxygen. Iron is your only ongoing cost.
HV
Still Early Game
Industry starting to ramp up.
Coal Flyash Processing FINISHED
ChainOptional
Coal combustion waste has gallium and germanium in it if you bother to look. Deep processing pulls both out as real outputs. Gallium for LEDs and semiconductors, germanium for optics and detector components. Turns something you were voiding into two useful materials.
EV
Mid Game
Assembly lines. Industrial chemicals. New multiblocks unlock.
Photoresist Chain: MV through EV FINISHED
ChainRequired
The start of a continuous photoresist chain that runs all the way to UMV. Every tier adds steps onto what's already there. Nothing from a previous tier stops running. The MV foundation produces Basic Photoresist for plastic circuit boards. HV upgrades it with distillation fractions and better sensitizers. EV introduces chemically amplified resist chemistry with a proper photo-acid generator.
RHEA Alloy and PPS Cables FINISHED
ChainRequired
Refractory High Entropy Alloy synthesis starting at EV. Five or more elements in roughly equimolar ratios. RHEA requires Polyphenylene sulfide in every recipe without exception. The final product is RHEA cables that gate IV machine hull construction, replacing tungsten cables across all IV hull recipes. Existing tungsten cable routes to IV hulls are removed.
  • PPS (Polyphenylene sulfide) required in all RHEA recipes. No shortcuts.
  • RHEA cables replace tungsten cables in IV machine hull recipes.
  • Existing tungsten wire routes to IV hull are removed from the game.
Tantalum-Niobium Separation FINISHED
ChainOptional
Coltan ore processing for high-purity tantalum and niobium. Solvent extraction splits the two metals which are nearly impossible to separate any other way. Tantalum for capacitors, niobium for superalloys. Quality of life ore chain.
Carbon Processing with Biomass Designing
ChainOptional
Carbon material processing using biological feedstocks. Biochar, activated carbon, and carbon precursors from biomass sources. Sets up infrastructure that feeds into higher-tier carbon chains later.
SiC for HTRF Casings FINISHED
ChainRequired
Silicon carbide synthesis at EV to produce the casings needed to build the HTRF. SiC is what gates the HTRF. The HTRF then opens up higher-temperature reactions and is required somewhere in IV progression.
High Temp Reaction Furnace (HTRF) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
2000K+ reactions the EBF can't reach. Gated by SiC casings. Required for IV progression. The HTRF is meant to be something you build and immediately feel like you need a second one.
Spectral Processing Chamber (SPC) FINISHED
MultiblockOptional
New EV multiblock for photon-assisted processing. Beam and frame module components allow different processing modes.
IV
Mid-Late Game
IV is perfect. Minimal additions.
Design Note

IV is intentionally light on new content. The main additions are the RHEA cable gate on machine hulls, photoresist continuing from EV, and the HTRF being required somewhere in IV progression. Nothing else. IV doesn't need fixing.

RHEA Cable Gate on IV Machine Hulls FINISHED
ChainRequired
IV machine hulls require RHEA cables instead of tungsten cables. The existing tungsten cable recipes for IV hulls are removed. RHEA cables require PPS polymer in production. This gates IV hull construction on having the full RHEA chain running from EV.
Photoresist Chain: IV Extension FINISHED
ChainRequired
IV introduces THP protection of the PHS resin using a crop-derived protecting group. Furfural from agricultural waste feeds directly into the chain. Crops finally have a meaningful chemical use beyond bioethanol.
LuV
Late Game
New multiblocks. Exotic materials. Void Miner content.
Vibranium Synthesis Designing
ChainRequired
5-step synthesis route derived from adamantium and something from the nether. Gates ZPM components. The exact nether source is still being decided but the output and gate are locked in.
Hexagonal Boron Nitride FINISHED
ChainRequired
hBN synthesis using the Ammonia Atmosphere Reactor. Primary use is LuV+ lubricant and lining the Ceramic Reaction Vessel. Also feeds into circuit substrates. The AAR is one of three LuV multiblocks you're building at this tier.
Unobtanium Designing
ChainRequired
9-step processing chain. Unobtanium is void miner only, found exclusively in The End dimension (not the End Farlands, just The End). Mining gives Unobtanium Ore Concentrate, one per ore. It can't be dissolved with normal acids and can't be melted the usual way so it needs a stronger approach. Balance will be tuned after playtesting.
Ammonia Atmosphere Reactor (AAR) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
High temperature and reactive NH3 atmosphere simultaneously. Required for hBN synthesis. Recipe fails if the ammonia supply drops. Continuous input machine.
High Pressure Sintering Furnace (HPSF) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
Heat and pressure simultaneously under inert atmosphere. Continuous inert gas required. Used for densification steps in SiC and hBN processing that can't happen in the EBF or HTRF.
Ceramic Reaction Vessel (CRV) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
hBN-lined interior. Only multiblock that can safely contain certain exotic molten metal mixtures. Required for Unobtanium processing and other LuV+ alloy synthesis that would destroy a normal crucible.
ZPM
Late Game cont.
Polymerization. Nuclear isotopes. Amorphous metals.
Nylon-6,6 FINISHED
ChainRequired
Condensation polymerization in the Polycondensation Vessel. Output feeds machine hulls, LATEX wire coating, and structural machine parts. Gates ZPM hull construction.
PLA Bio-Mulch FINISHED
ChainOptional
Polylactic acid from fermentation, polymerized in the PCV. Feeds ore processing multiblocks, filtration membranes, and biofuel systems. PLA fermentation improves bioethanol yield as a side effect.
Promethium and Promethic-Naquadria Designing
ChainRequired
Reactor-based isotope synthesis for promethium and its naquadria compound. Promethium feeds into betavoltaic cell production. Promethic-Naquadria is a ZPM-tier material with uses in high-tier machines and components.
Amorphous Tritanium Alloy Designing
ChainRequired
Amorphous (non-crystalline) Tritanium alloy produced at ZPM. Required alongside Americium in UV motor construction. Gates UV motors on having a working ZPM amorphous metal production line.
Photoresist Chain: ZPM Blend FINISHED
ChainRequired
ZPM introduces fluorinated polymer chemistry and three-monomer copolymerization for 193nm ArF lithography. Gamma-butyrolactone from the Kevlar chain connects the photoresist line to one of GTNH's hardest existing chains. The chain keeps feeding itself across tiers.
Polycondensation Vessel (PCV) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
Vacuum-assisted condensation polymerization. Required for Nylon-6,6 and PLA. Runs both chains and stays busy throughout ZPM.
UV
Late-Endgame Entry
Wetware circuits. Carbon fiber completion. New modules.
Kapton / Polyimide for Wetware Boards FINISHED
ChainRequired
Kapton film production via PFC casting and imidization. Required for UV wetware circuit boards. Two-step process: cast the polyamic acid film in the PFC, then thermally cure it at 300C+ in imidization mode.
Carbon Fiber Composite FINISHED
ChainRequired
The carbon fiber chain completes at UV using the Dual Atmosphere Furnace. Stabilization in oxidizing mode, carbonization in inert mode. The finished composite was needed for ZPM-tier builds and the chain closes here. Replaces basic carbon fiber in higher-tier recipes.
Amorphous Metal Components Designing
ChainRequired
Amorphous metals used as UV motor and structural components. Ties into the Amorphous Tritanium chain from ZPM and expands into new amorphous alloy compositions at UV.
Grade 1 Water Filter Improvement Designing
ChainOptional
A much more efficient route to Grade 1 water filters using UV-tier chemistry. Significantly reduces the resource cost of filtered water production at scale.
New Module Recipes Designing
ChainOptional
New recipes for existing UV module systems using materials from the chains now running at UV. Exact modules to be decided during implementation.
Photoresist Chain: UV Blend FINISHED
ChainRequired
UV completely changes the chemistry class. Rare earth metal doped tin clusters replace the fluorinated polymer approach from ZPM. Four exotic rare earths each do something chemically distinct. Requires the Controlled Isotopic Doping Chamber.
Controlled Isotopic Doping Chamber (CIDC) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
Precisely dopes tin oxo clusters with rare earth compounds under controlled conditions. Required for UV photoresist production.
Precision Film Caster (PFC) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
Two modes: casting (film formation) and imidization (thermal cure at 300C+). Required for Kapton film and other precision polymer films.
Dual Atmosphere Furnace (DAF) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
Two modes: oxidizing (air/O2) for stabilization, inert (N2/Ar) for carbonization. Switch with screwdriver. Required for carbon fiber composite completion.
UHV
Endgame
Bio-hybrid chemistry. Aerogel for multiblock construction.
Photoresist Chain: UHV Extension FINISHED
ChainRequired
UHV is the first tier where biological components enter the chain. Bio-hybrid resist chemistry. Radox Polymer and Xenoxene from mandatory UHV lines become the new matrix. Needs the Hybrid Phase Reactor.
Aerogel for Multiblock Construction Designing
ChainRequired
Aerogel insulation casings used in UHV multiblock construction. Silica aerogel is the base. 2-4 additional aerogel variants planned with distinct identities and uses, not just reskins. The SCD handles supercritical drying. Exact variant count and uses still being designed.
Cryogenic Aerogel Chamber (CAC) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
Aerogel-insulated reactor for cryogenic and extreme-temperature processes. Aerogel insulation casings are consumed in UHV multiblock construction.
Hybrid Phase Reactor (HPR) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
Handles biological and inorganic chemistry simultaneously in the same vessel. Required for UHV photoresist production.
Supercritical Dryer (SCD) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
241C, 63 bar. Kills surface tension during drying. Ethanol recovery loop built in. Required for aerogel production without pore collapse.
UEV
Endgame cont.
Quantum cascade chemistry.
Photoresist Chain: UEV Extension FINISHED
ChainRequired
UEV introduces quantum electron cascade chemistry. Individual photons trigger electron avalanches at the attosecond timescale. The difficulty is the machines not the materials. QFT Tier 1 appears twice, the Beamcrafter fires the 92 eV photon beam. Materials are all accessible early UEV.
UIV
Deep Endgame
Quantum field interactions. No more chemistry.
Photoresist Chain: UIV Extension FINISHED
ChainRequired
UIV drops chemistry entirely. The resist works through quantum field interactions. QGP from the Forge of the Gods Heliofusion Exoticizer is the active patterning medium. The Subatomic Patterning Unit appears three times and automating it properly is the real challenge.
Subatomic Patterning Unit (SPU) FINISHED
MultiblockRequired
Manipulates quantum fields directly. Works below the atomic scale. Appears 3 times in UIV photoresist chain. Automating it is the challenge, not the materials.
UMV / UXV / MAX
Endergame
The final stretch. The time wall.
Photoresist Chain: UMV Extension FINISHED
ChainRequired
UMV is the time wall. The photoresist chain here isn't designed to be hard, it's designed to keep players busy while the Eye of Harmony grinds away. 18 steps using non-EoH materials. Some steps need absurd quantities of bulk materials. Some are surprisingly normal. The cumulative cost is staggering and that's the point.
The Required Line Designing
ChainRequired
A large required processing line at UMV that demands several passive sub-setups running continuously. Long enough to fill the time between EoH cycles with actual work. Scope and materials to be designed.

Chemistry Chains

Processing chains with distinct chemical identities, multiple game uses, and connections to at least two other things.

Circuit Substrate Ladder

This mod completes a circuit substrate progression: Polyethylene (existing, MV-EV) to Kapton film (IV-UV) to Hexagonal Boron Nitride (LuV+). Each material is justified for its tier.

Interdependency Web

RHEA gates IV hulls. SiC gates the HTRF. Kapton feeds the PFC. hBN feeds the CRV and lubricant systems. Carbon Fiber completes in the DAF. Aerogel lines UHV multiblock casings. Everything touches at least two other things.

06

Future Updates

Core multiblocks and chains are implemented. Remaining work is balancing, filling in chain details, and the items below.

Still Designing

BOF Recipes and Balance

BOF structure is built. Recipes and the three carbon mode balance still being worked out.

Vibranium Nether Source

Vibranium is 5 steps from adamantium plus a nether-sourced ingredient. The exact nether material isn't decided yet.

Aerogel Variants

2-4 additional aerogel types beyond silica. Exact count and uses to be decided. All will have distinct applications, not just reskins.

UXV / MAX Photoresist

Final two tiers of the photoresist chain. Design pending until earlier tiers are verified in gameplay.

UMV Required Line

The big required chain at UMV that keeps players busy between EoH cycles. Scope and materials to be designed.

Permanently Out of Scope

No parallel routes to materials that already have chains in base GTNH. No world gen changes. No difficulty overhauls. New chains produce genuinely new outputs. Existing GTNH materials only appear as inputs, never as competing outputs.
