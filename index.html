01

Overview

GT:NH Processing+ is an expansion addon for GregTech: New Horizons. It's not trying to fix GTNH or change what it is. GTNH is already great. This mod just adds more of what it already does well: new processing chains, new machines, and mechanics that reward players who go deeper without punishing everyone else.

There's content at every tier. Some of it is optional and rewards players who build it. Some of it gates progression the way GTNH naturally does. All of it should feel like it belongs in the pack.

Core Principle

Every piece of content should make a player feel like their effort was worth it. Something new greets you at each tier. Nothing exists just to add steps.

What this mod is

More of what GTNH does. New chains using existing machines. New machines that solve problems players already feel. Content that feels like it was always supposed to be there.

What this mod is not

A difficulty overhaul. A world gen mod. A replacement for anything that already exists. Processing+ runs alongside GTNH, not against it.

02

Design Philosophy

These principles drive every design decision. Content that satisfies none of them doesn't belong. There's no strict checklist, use judgment the same way GTNH's devs do.

01
Branching Routes

No single optimal path. Every branch has different costs and tradeoffs. The best choice changes as your base evolves.

02
Byproducts Matter

No output is throwaway. Every byproduct should have a use somewhere in this mod or in base GTNH.

03
Infrastructure Pressure

Content is designed to be undersized when first built. The game rewards players who expand, not players who stop at the minimum.

04
Layered Depth

New tiers add layers to existing content. Early infrastructure stays relevant and useful later.

05
Named Lines

Critical materials graduate to dedicated continuous pipelines with internal loops. The platline model.

06
Diverse Challenges

Not every piece of content is a throughput problem. Some test layout, some test automation, some test routing. Variety matters.

07
Web of Dependencies

Nothing stands alone. Everything touches at least two other things as a consumer, a producer, or both.

08
Grounded Logic

Reactions and processes are based in real industrial logic. The game simplifies heavily but the reasoning should still feel satisfying.

On Difficulty

Difficulty comes from infrastructure demands, not step count. A three-step chain requiring a new multiblock and two continuous inputs is harder than a ten-step chain in one machine. Respect the difficulty GTNH already has, don't undercut it or pile on top of it.

On Tier Transitions

Every tier transition should have a moment. Something new that greets the player and makes the grind feel worth it. Content at each tier should solve problems players already feel, not create new arbitrary ones.

Content by Tier

This is the living part of the document. Each tier gets its own block and features get added as they're fully designed. Status tags show where each feature is at.

Steam / LV
Early Game
Before the EBF. The grind before electricity takes off.
Basic Oxygen Furnace (BOF) Designing
MultiblockOptional
A small LV multiblock that converts hot charged iron into steel using a continuous oxygen lance. Cheaper than the EBF but better at steel specifically so it stays relevant even after EBF is online. Solves the pre-EBF steel bottleneck without removing the challenge of getting there.
  • Cheaper to build than EBF. You can get it running before EBF materials are even stockpiled.
  • Needs continuous oxygen from an electrolyzer. Actual infrastructure investment.
  • Three carbon content modes via Alloy Smelter preheating. Each with real tradeoffs.
  • Stays permanently relevant as a steel specialist even after EBF is online.
Iron Preheating and Carbon Tuning Designing
ChainOptional
The prep step that feeds the BOF. Iron ingots plus varying amounts of carbon dust in the Alloy Smelter give you hot charged iron with three different carbon levels. Each feeds into the BOF with different results. The choice matters and stays relevant for the whole playthrough.
  • Low carbon: fastest BOF, highest steel per time. Wasteful but rapid.
  • Standard carbon: balanced output, the default if you don't want to think about it.
  • High carbon: 3-4x slower but fully recycles all carbon and oxygen. Self-sustaining steel loop where iron is your only ongoing cost.
  • Uses the Alloy Smelter. Something players already have running.
HV
Mid-Early Game
Cleanroom, space travel, moon ores, first multiblocks.
Moon-Forged Concrete Line Planned
ChainRequired
A structural material made entirely from moon-derived inputs. Not required for base GTNH progression but gates all Processing+ multiblocks specifically. Gives three underused moon ore byproducts a critical purpose and keeps the Implosion Compressor running long after the rocket is built. Comes in two tiers, Compressed Lunar Aggregate for early Processing+ multiblocks and Moon-Forged Concrete for advanced ones.
  • Moon Dust → Olivine (centrifuge, chance output, already exists in GTNH)
  • Uvarovite + Sulfuric Acid → Chromium Silicate solution + Calcium dust (Chemical Bath, HV)
  • Olivine + Chromium Silicate solution + Molybdenum dust → Compressed Lunar Aggregate (Implosion Compressor, HV)
  • Compressed Lunar Aggregate → Moon-Forged Concrete (HTRF, ZPM)
  • Compressed Lunar Aggregate used as foundation layer for early Processing+ multiblocks
  • Moon-Forged Concrete used as foundation layer for advanced Processing+ multiblocks
MV
Early-Mid Game
First real machines. Steel becomes abundant. Chemical complexity begins.
Photoresist Chain: MV Foundation Planned
ChainRequired
The start of a single continuous photoresist chain that runs from MV all the way to UXV. Every tier adds steps onto what's already there. Nothing from a previous tier stops running. The MV foundation produces Basic Photoresist fluid which gets used in plastic circuit board recipes and adds actual depth to what was previously a really simple process.
  • Ethanol + O2 → Formaldehyde
  • Phenol + Formaldehyde + H2SO4 (recycled) → Novolac Resin
  • Benzene + O2 → Benzoquinone OR Wood + Water → Tannin Sensitizer (two routes depending on what you have set up)
  • Novolac Resin + Sensitizer + Ethanol → Basic Photoresist fluid
  • Basic Photoresist fluid used in plastic circuit board recipes
Photoresist Chain: HV Extension Planned
ChainRequired
HV unlocks the Distillation Tower which gives you naphthalene and anthracene. Both are better sensitizers than what MV had access to. Three alternate routes to the sensitizer, all producing the same Advanced Photoresist fluid. Basic Photoresist from MV gets consumed continuously as a base input.
  • Naphthalene → Beta-naphthol → DNQ Sensitizer (main route, best quality)
  • Anthracene → Anthraquinone Sensitizer (alternate DT fraction)
  • Benzene → Resorcinol Sensitizer (for players deep in benzene chemistry)
  • Basic Photoresist fluid + Sensitizer → Advanced Photoresist fluid
EV / IV
Mid Game
Industrial buildup. Assembly lines. The complexity spike before LuV.
Photoresist Chain: EV Extension Planned
ChainRequired
EV introduces polyhydroxystyrene resin and a proper photo-acid generator. The chain moves from dissolution inhibitor chemistry to chemically amplified resist chemistry. Advanced Photoresist from HV gets consumed continuously as the base input.
  • Styrene + Acetic Anhydride → Acetoxystyrene
  • Acetoxystyrene + H2O2 + HCl (recycled) → PHS Resin
  • Sulfur + Chlorine + Benzene → Diphenylsulfonium Salt (PAG)
  • Advanced Photoresist + PHS Resin + PAG → EV Photoresist fluid
Photoresist Chain: IV Extension Planned
ChainRequired
IV introduces THP protection of the PHS resin using a crop-derived protecting group. Furfural from agricultural waste feeds directly into the chain. Crops finally have a meaningful chemical use beyond bioethanol.
  • Crops + H2SO4 → Furfural
  • Furfural + Heat → Dihydropyran + CO byproduct
  • PHS Resin + Dihydropyran + HCl (recycled) → THP Protected PHS
  • EV Photoresist + THP Protected PHS → IV Photoresist fluid
Pollucite Processing Line Planned
Ore ChainOptional
Pollucite is a caesium aluminium silicate found in mica veins. Deep processing via acid digestion gives way more caesium than standard electrolyzing, with rubidium as a real secondary output instead of a trace byproduct. Ends with caesium formate, a better high density drilling fluid.
  • LCR acid digestion → caesium chloride solution, rubidium chloride, aluminium chloride, silicon dioxide
  • Caesium chloride + potassium permanganate → high purity caesium dust
  • Rubidium becomes a real output not just a trace byproduct
  • Caesium formate: new material, better drilling fluid at IV+. Caesium's main real-world industrial use.
LuV / ZPM
Late Game
The complexity spike. Named lines become necessary.
Photoresist Chain: LuV Extension Planned
ChainRequired
LuV is the complexity spike of the photoresist chain. 12 steps including a triflic acid sub-chain. Introduces alicyclic polymer chemistry that ditches the aromatic backbone entirely. HCN from the Carbon Fiber chain, Adamantanol from Naquadah, and fluorine from the PTFE chain all converge here. The chain starts feeding itself across tiers.
  • Chloroform + HF → Trifluoromethane + SO2 + O2 → Triflic Acid (sub-chain)
  • Acetone + HCN + H2SO4 → Methacrylic Acid (HCN comes from CF composite chain)
  • Methacrylic Acid + Adamantanol → Adamantyl Methacrylate (Adamantanol from Naquadah)
  • Acetone + Hydrazine → Acetone Azine + HCN + Chlorine → AIBN initiator
  • Adamantyl Methacrylate + Methacrylic Acid + AIBN + Nitrogen → Alicyclic Resin
  • Diphenylsulfonium Salt (EV) + Triflic Acid → Triphenylsulfonium Triflate (stronger PAG)
  • Propylene Oxide + Methanol → PGME + Acetic Acid → PGMEA solvent
  • IV Photoresist + Alicyclic Resin + Triphenylsulfonium Triflate + PGMEA → LuV Photoresist
Photoresist Chain: ZPM Extension Planned
ChainRequired
ZPM introduces fluorinated polymer chemistry and three-monomer copolymerization for 193nm ArF lithography. Gamma-butyrolactone from the Kevlar chain connects the photoresist line to one of GTNH's hardest existing chains. AIBN and PGMEA get reused from LuV. The chain keeps feeding itself.
  • Acetone + Fluorine → Hexafluoroacetone (new material)
  • Hexafluoroacetone + Methacrylic Acid → HFIMA fluorinated monomer
  • Gamma-Butyrolactone (Kevlar chain) + Methacrylic Acid → GBLMA monomer
  • Adamantanol + Methacrylic Acid + H2O2 → HAdMA monomer
  • GBLMA + HAdMA + HFIMA + AIBN (LuV) + Nitrogen → ArF Copolymer Resin
  • LuV Photoresist + ArF Copolymer Resin + PGMEA (LuV) → ZPM Photoresist
Chemistry Chains v1.0 In Development
ChainRequired
Seven new material processing chains from LuV through UHV. Each has a distinct chemical identity, multiple game uses, and connects to at least two other chains. Full detail in the Chemistry Chains section.
  • Nylon-6,6: machine hulls, LATEX wire coating, structural
  • Carbon Fiber Composite: structural, turbine speed boost, CRC casings
  • Silicon Carbide: gates HTRF, ABS upgrade, machine hulls
  • Kapton / Polyimide: UV circuit gate, precision multiblock casings
  • Polylactic Acid: bio-based, ore processing multiblocks, biofuel bonus
  • Hexagonal Boron Nitride: LuV+ lubricant, CRV multiblock, circuit substrates
  • Silica Aerogel: CRC catalyst support, insulation panels
UV / UHV
Endgame Entry
Wetware circuits. Space elevator. Mega multiblocks.
Photoresist Chain: UV Extension Planned
ChainRequired
UV completely changes the chemistry class. Rare earth metal doped tin clusters replace the fluorinated polymer approach from ZPM. Four exotic rare earths each do something chemically distinct. Needs the new Controlled Isotopic Doping Chamber multiblock.
  • Tin + Chlorine → Tin Tetrachloride → Tin Oxo Acetate Cluster scaffold
  • Erbium + Triflic Acid → Erbium Triflate (photocatalyst, absorbs UV photons)
  • Ytterbium + Acetic Acid → Ytterbium Acetate (sensitizer, transfers energy to erbium)
  • Terbium + HCl → Terbium Chloride → Terbium Acetylacetonate (resolution enhancer)
  • Dysprosium + CaF2 + HCl → Dysprosium-doped CaF2 (radiation sensitizer)
  • Tin Oxo Cluster + all four RE compounds → RE-Doped Matrix (Controlled Isotopic Doping Chamber)
  • ZPM Photoresist + RE Matrix + PGMEA → UV Photoresist
Photoresist Chain: UHV Extension Planned
ChainRequired
UHV is the first tier where biological components enter the chain. Bio-hybrid resist chemistry. Radox Polymer and Xenoxene from mandatory UHV lines become the new matrix. Needs the new Hybrid Phase Reactor multiblock.
  • UV Photoresist + Mutagen + Unknown Liquid → Bio-Refined Intermediate
  • Radox Polymer + Xenoxene → Radox-Xenoxene Matrix (Hybrid Phase Reactor)
  • Mutated Living Solder + Acetic Acid → Living Solder Acetate
  • Bio-Refined Intermediate + Radox-Xenoxene Matrix + Living Solder Acetate → UHV Matrix
  • UHV Matrix + PGMEA → UHV Photoresist
UEV / UIV
Deep Endgame
Optical circuits. Exotic planets. Transcendent materials.
Photoresist Chain: UEV Extension Planned
ChainRequired
UEV introduces quantum electron cascade chemistry. Individual photons trigger electron avalanches at the attosecond timescale. The difficulty is the machines not the materials. QFT Tier 1 appears twice, the Beamcrafter fires the 92 eV photon beam. Materials are all accessible early UEV.
  • Tengam + Triflic Acid + Grade 7 Water → Tengam Triflate
  • Naquadria + Nitric Acid + Grade 7 Water → Activated Naquadria solution
  • Hypogen + Grade 7 Water → Hypogen Quantum Matrix (Fusion Mk4)
  • Fermium Plasma + Triflic Acid → Fermium Triflate
  • UHV Photoresist + Tengam Triflate + Grade 7 Water → Quantum Primed Intermediate (QFT)
  • Quantum Primed + 92 eV beam → Beam Activated Intermediate (Beamcrafter)
  • Beam Activated + Activated Naquadria → Naquadria Loaded Intermediate
  • Naquadria Loaded + Hypogen Matrix + Fermium Triflate → Quantum Cascade Matrix (QFT)
  • Quantum Cascade Matrix + Grade 7 Water → Purified Matrix + radioactive waste
  • Purified Matrix + PGMEA → UEV Photoresist
Photoresist Chain: UIV Extension Planned
ChainRequired
UIV drops chemistry entirely. The resist works through quantum field interactions. QGP from the Forge of the Gods Heliofusion Exoticizer is the active patterning medium. The Subatomic Patterning Unit appears three times and automating it properly is the real challenge.
  • Heliofusion Exoticizer (Forge of the Gods) → Quark-Gluon Plasma
  • QGP + Graviton Shards + Grade 7 Water → Stabilized QGP Matrix (Subatomic Patterning Unit)
  • Transcendent Metal + Stabilized QGP Matrix → Transcendent QGP Lattice (SPU)
  • Creon + Triflic Acid + Grade 7 Water → Creon Triflate (LCR)
  • Transcendent QGP Lattice + Creon Triflate → Quantum Field Imprinted Intermediate (SPU)
  • Quantum Field Imprinted Intermediate + UEV Photoresist → UIV Matrix
  • UIV Matrix + PGMEA → UIV Photoresist

The Absolute End
The final stretch. Almost nobody gets here. Content should feel legendary.

ChainRequired
UMV is the time wall. The photoresist chain here isn't designed to be hard, it's designed to keep players busy while the Eye of Harmony grinds away. 18 steps using non-EoH materials. Some steps need absurd quantities of bulk materials. Some are surprisingly normal. The cumulative cost is staggering and that's the point.

  • 32x Cosmic Neutronium + Triflic Acid → Cosmic Neutronium Triflate

Chemistry Chains

The seven v1.0 chemistry chains. Full recipe detail is in a separate reference doc. This section covers identity, purpose, and how they connect.

Circuit Substrate Ladder

This mod completes a circuit substrate progression across all tiers: Polyethylene (existing, MV-EV) → Kapton film (IV) → Hexagonal Boron Nitride (LuV/ZPM). Each material is justified for its tier.

Interdependency Web

Kapton feeds aerogel (v1.1). PLA fermentation improves bioethanol as a side effect. CF composite casings needed for CRC. SiC gates HTRF. HTRF needs hBN lubricant to run at full efficiency. hBN CRV and lubricant equals sustained endgame demand. Aerogel CRC unlocks advanced chemistry that benefits every other chain.

Future Updates

v1.0 ships with the seven chemistry chains and the BOF steel system. Future versions fill in the tiers still marked in progress and expand existing chains.

v1.1: Aerogel Variants

Carbon Aerogel

Electrically conductive aerogel for supercapacitor and energy storage components. Different feedstock, different electrical identity from silica aerogel.

Polyimide Aerogel

Uses the Kapton PAA intermediate as a gel precursor. Higher tier thermal insulation. Directly connects Kapton and Aerogel chains. Players who built both are already halfway there.

v1.2: HV/EV/IV Content

Content for the mid-game tiers currently marked in progress. Scope to be determined as tier-by-tier design conversations finish up.

Permanently Out of Scope

No parallel routes to materials that already have chains in base GTNH. No world gen changes. No difficulty overhauls. New chains produce genuinely new outputs. Existing GTNH materials only ever appear as inputs, never as competing outputs.
